uberX requires a vehicle inspection as a part of the onboarding process. You can start driving immediately once activated, but you must pass an inspection within 30 days after activating your uberX account.

What to do:

  1. Check the list (found on http://t.uber.com/sfinspection) to find a participating auto shop closer to you. Call ahead of time to schedule an appointment with these auto shops to save time.
  2. Print out the inspection form (found on http://t.uber.com/sfinspection). Go get your inspection done. You must pass an inspection within 30 days after activating your uberX account.
  3. Take a picture of both the inspection form and receipt. Upload the picture to http://partners.uber.com or email the picture to sfdocs@uber.com.
  4. If you have any questions, please go to http://t.uber.com/sfinspection.

Uber has partnered with Toyota and GM to offer discounts on new vehicles and help with financing on new and used vehicles. Only Uber partners are eligible and program benefits include:

  • No credit needed for approval
  • Discounted APR
  • Low money down
  • Max 4-year loan term
  • Make payments through Uber trips

Go to http://t.uber.com/financeSF for more information on how to become eligible.

*Financing by Exeter Finance Corp. Not all applicants will qualify for this special program. Exeter Finance Corp. has set the requirements for their program and certain items present on your credit report may cause you to be excluded from this offer, including—without limitation—a repossessed vehicle within the last 12 months, a pending bankruptcy, or a discharged bankruptcy within the last 12 months.
